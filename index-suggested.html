About Bass Invaders:


Four coders, Merika Toby, Chelsea Pratte, Hannah Middleton and Brian Lu have teamed up to build and introduce users to a breathtaking fusion of music and technology! Our innovation combines multiple restful APIs, including YouTube and ChatGPT, to bring you a personalized song search like never before. Experience the thrill of finding perfect tunes for your mood or activities, accompanied by captivating YouTube videos. And fear not, our enchanting creation ensures a seamless journey, with the whimsical presence of the Easter bunny to vanquish any glitches. Prepare for an extraordinary musical adventure, where every moment resonates with grandeur!
